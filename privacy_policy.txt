Privacy Policy for ProFollow App

Last Updated: January 2025

ProFollow (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy.
This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect
The app may collect the following information ONLY when you manually enter it:
- Name
- Phone Number
- Address
- Business Details (customer/vendor entries, items, bills, GST, etc.)

This information is saved only on your device inside a secure local database (SQLite).

2. Device Permissions
The app may request the following permissions:

a) READ_CONTACTS (Optional)
Used only when you manually pick a contact to auto-fill customer/vendor/Staff phone number.
The app never reads your full contact list automatically and never uploads contacts anywhere.

b) Storage Access
Used only for:
- Generating PDF bills
- Saving PDF files locally

We do not access or upload any files.

3. How Your Data Is Used
All data is stored locally on your device and is used only for:
- Billing
- Customer management
- Inventory
- Reports

We do NOT:
- Collect data in the background
- Store data on our servers
- Sell or share data with third parties
- Upload business or personal data

4. Data Sharing
We do not share any personal or business data with anyone.

5. Data Security
- All data is stored locally in SQLite on your device.
- No data leaves the device.
- Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will delete all stored information.

6. Children's Privacy
The app is not intended for children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy as needed.
Users will be notified inside the Play Store listing if major changes occur.
